The Studio

Where Mythology
Meets Mechanics

Yehnuu was born from a simple belief: that role-playing games are the most powerful storytelling medium ever devised. Not just interactive narratives — living worlds with memory, consequence, and soul.

We are a small, fiercely dedicated team of world-builders, writers, and systems designers. Every game we make begins with a question: what if this world were real?

From that question, we build outward — histories, religions, ecologies, languages — until the mechanics themselves feel like ancient laws of nature.

Our Craft

Three Pillars of Design

I
Deep World-Building

Every map has borders beyond the frame. Every NPC has a life before you arrived. Our worlds breathe whether or not the player is watching.

II
Consequential Choice

Decisions matter at every scale — from how you greet a beggar to which empire you help ascend. Moral weight is woven into the system itself.

III
Mythic Tone

We write in the register of legend. Our stories deal in fate, sacrifice, and transformation. We believe games can carry the weight of great literature.
