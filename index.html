The Studio

Yehnuu

An independent RPG studio forging worlds of mythic weight and moral consequence.

We believe the greatest games are the most honest — honest about darkness, about the cost of power, about what it means to choose.

I — World
Breathing Cosmologies

Our worlds have histories before the first quest begins. Gods died here. Empires fell. The dust still holds their names. Every map edge hides another age waiting to be uncovered.

II — Choice
Weight Without Escape

We do not make choices comfortable. Every decision carries a ledger. Alliances break. Silences cost lives. The system remembers what you wish it would forget.

III — Darkness
Honest Shadows

We write darkness not as aesthetic but as truth. Grief, complicity, power's corruption — our games go where literature goes, holding nothing back from the player who chooses to look.

IV — Craft
Systems as Language

Mechanics are not separate from narrative. In Tenebraia, feeding the Veil is also how you survive. The loop is the metaphor.

✠
We do not offer you escape. We offer you descent — and the chance, if you are brave enough, to discover what you truly are in the dark.
